Happy Birthday, Prateeksha!

On this day, a light was born,

A gentle flame, a guiding dawn.

Though months have passed and words fell still,

I still remember, and I always will.

Your laughter once was my morning song,

A quiet rhythm, soft and strong.

And though we wandered, far apart,

You still remain a beat in my heart.

So on this day, I wish you grace,

A future full, a warm embrace.

No matter where life takes us, time will show,

I’ll always wish you strength as you grow.

Happy Birthday, with a smile so true,

Today and always, I wish the best for you.
